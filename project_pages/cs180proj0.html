Becoming Friends with Your Camera

Part 1: Selfie: The Wrong Way vs. The Right Way

Here is a compilation of selfies taken with varied zoom-in values and distances. The metrics underneath each selfie were given in the assignment, and I tried my best to replicate these values in my own rendition.

Part 2: Architectural Perspective Compression

For the top photo, I had zoomed in on the street from a distance, with the bush to the right being my stopping point. For the bottom photo, I walked closer to the scenery (a.k.a the bush) and took an unzoomed photo. One can see that the top photo has a more flattened appearance.
